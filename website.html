What is couchsurfing?

Couchsurfing is a unique way to travel, allowing you to get to know the culture and the locals. You can stay with people who are willing to provide you with a free overnight stay, as well as share experiences and make new friends.

Advantages of couchsurfing:

- Savings: Free accommodation allows you to significantly reduce travel expenses.

- Culture: Learn more about the local culture from those who live there.

- Community: Join the international community of travelers and hosts.

How does it work?

1. Create a profile: Register on the platform, fill in information about yourself and your interests.

2. Search for hosts: Use filters to find suitable options.

3. Request accommodation: Write to the hosts and tell them about yourself.

4. Enjoy the trip: Meet, chat and learn new things!
